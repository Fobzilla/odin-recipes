Chilli Noodles

Here's everthing you need to make this delicious dish!

Perfect for cold weather!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chilli crisp oil
  • Garlic
  • Spring onions

Steps

  1. Combine chilli crisp oil, garlic and chopped spring onion in a frying pan
  2. Cook noodles until just softened
  3. combine in pan and cook on medium until combined
  4. add some pasta water to thicken the sauce
