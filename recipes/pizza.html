Pizza

A simple pizza recipe anyone can make

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato pasta
  • Oregano
  • Optional: Chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Combine tomato paste and oregano
  2. Spread paste over pizza base
  3. Add slices of pepperoni
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Cook for 10-15mins at 200 deg
